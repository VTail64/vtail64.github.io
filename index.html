About Me

Hello! I'm VTail, a developer and VRChat world creator focused on aviation experiences. I strive to create immersive, highly detailed worlds that encompass the excitement of flight for anyone to enjoy.

My goal for these VRC worlds is to raise the bar for realism and immersion typically not seen on the platform. By expanding upon existing code, implementing new features, meticulously refinining values and parameters, these worlds are designed to be the best aviation experiences available in VRChat.

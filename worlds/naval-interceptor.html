About This World

Climb aboard the Harry S. Truman aircraft carrier and reach into the sky with the legendary F-14A Tomcat. Enjoy the scenery, or fight friends or foes in either the sky or ground. Or fly around in additional vehicles such as the MiG-23MLA Flogger, or SH-3 Sea King.

  • One of the most advanced flyable aircraft in VRChat.
  • Ground SAM/AAA NPCs to provide active enemy threats.
  • Customizable aircraft liveries.

This world features my most detailed and advanced set of aircraft as of yet, encompassing over 3 years of development and tons of refinement for both the flight handling, world features, and additional scripts that put these aircraft above and beyond what other worlds can offer.

  • Engine code overhaul with per-engine calculations.
  • Wing sweep system that affects flight dynamics.
  • Wing stress mechanic, including overstress separation.
  • Overhauled immersive audio system.
